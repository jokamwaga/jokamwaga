About Us

Who We Are

Joka Mwaga is more than a savings group — we are a family bound by trust, transparency, and a shared vision for financial independence and mutual care.

Welcome to our family group — a home for all descendants of our beloved grandparent, Marikus Mwaga Akumu. This platform unites us in love, respect, togetherness, and family pride. May we strengthen our bond, support one another, preserve our heritage, and celebrate every milestone as one family. Feel free to interact, share memories, ideas, and important updates. Together, we remain stronger. You can make it better.

Our Mission

To foster a culture of disciplined saving, smart investing, and compassionate emergency support among our family members.

Our Vision

A financially empowered family where every member thrives through collective effort, transparency, and mutual accountability.

Our Values

Trust, Transparency, Unity, Discipline, and Mutual Care — these principles guide every contribution and every decision we make.
